About Vex

VEX Robotics

VEX Robotics provides educational robotics solutions for all levels of both formal and informal education with accessible, scalable, and affordable solutions. Beyond science and engineering principles, VEX encourages creativity, teamwork, leadership, and problem solving among groups. It allows educators of all types to engage and inspire the STEM problem solvers of tomorrow! VEX also hosts competitions that foster these skills and capitalize on the motivational effects of competitions and robotics to help all students create an identity as a STEM learner.

VEX Robotics Competition (VRC)

In the VEX Robotics Competition, teams (seperated by middle-school, high-school and university) are tasked with designing and building a robot to play against other teams in a game-based engineering challenge. In this year's VRC challenge, called "Change Up", teams are tasked with stacking the right coloured balls in goals and playing a sort-of 3D tic-tac-toe.
